# Welcome & Onboarding Flow

## Goal

Convert SaaS trial users into paying customers by driving activation in the first 7 days.

## Strategy

- Trigger: New signup
- Segments: Active vs inactive users
- Channels: Email
- KPIs: Activation %, Conversion %, CTR
